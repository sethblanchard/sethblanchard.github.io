Malleable

I've taken many shapes in my life and career: Mathematician (never seriously), Programmer, Automation Specialist, Climber, Missionary, User Experience Designer, Carpenter, Visualization Specialist, Teacher. I love finding new forms. Stretching for understanding, settling into new molds, always growing, never still.

Even while changing form into a new discipline, the imprints of the earlier roles never fade. These imprints inform your new work while helping you relate to those in positions you previously held.
